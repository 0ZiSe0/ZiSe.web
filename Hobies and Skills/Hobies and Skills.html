Drawing

I have been drawing since I knew how to pick up a pencil. I started taking drawing classes when i was 10. I stopped occationaly, but i would always pick up the pencil and start doing some lines.

I am currently studying industrial product design at the Polytechnical University of Catalonia (UPC), but i am rethinking of dropping out and starting this journey and make it my dream job.


Painting

My first ever painting was an oil painting. A landscape full of trees. It wasn't that good but it was a start. I started doing paint at the same time I applied at my drawing classes. It was mostly acrylic painting.

I tried different types of paint such as water based paint and also sand painting.

Thanks to this skill I will be able to apply it to my crafts and add some unique touches to it.


Sculpting

I am reletively new to this hobby. I started just a year ago and haven't honed this skill yet. It is a very entertaining skill to play with. I am working on towards doing digital sculpting using Blender and other softwares.

I will implement all my knowlage about it to print out my ideas on my 3D printer.
